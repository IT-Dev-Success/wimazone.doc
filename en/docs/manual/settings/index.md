# Global Settings

The **Settings** area centralizes global platform configuration.

## Main pages

- `Settings > General` (`/settings` or `/settings/general`)
- `Settings > Colors` (`/settings/colors`)
- `Settings > APIs` (`/settings/apis`)
- `Settings > SMTP` (`/settings/smtp`)
- `Settings > Payments` (`/settings/payments`)
- `Settings > SMS` (`/settings/sms`)
- `Settings > Customer Portal Plugins` (`/settings/customer-portal/plugins`)
- `Settings > Ticket Print Templates` (`/settings/ticket-print/templates`)

## Permissions

- Global access: `manage-settings`
- Sensitive actions (APIs, plugins/templates): usually `Super Admin`

## Best practices

1. Apply critical changes during off-peak hours.
2. Test on one pilot router before global rollout.
3. Keep an audit trail of major changes.
