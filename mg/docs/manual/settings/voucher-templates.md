# Modely Tikety

Ny modely fanontana tikety dia tantanina ao amin'ny:

- `/settings/ticket-print/templates`

## Asa

- mametraka modely ZIP,
- alefaso/ajanony,
- sync paramètre,
- mijery modely mialoha,
- manavao/mamafa.

## Laharan'ny fanehoana

1. modely mavitrika,
2. override mavitrika (raha voapetraka),
3. modely fallback default.

## Fanao tsara indrindra

- tehirizo modely fallback tsotra,
- soroka ny asset mavesatra,
- tsapao ny `vouchers/print` sy `vouchers/print-multiple` aorian'ny fanovana modely.
